About Dawood's Meat Co.

Dawood’s Meat Company are manufacturers of delicious and nutritious ready to cook products made from only the highest quality locally sourced ingredients. Their products range from fresh meat, including whole chicken, to tandoori and 3-pepper marinated meats, grilled smokies, chicken wings, legs and thighs.

Family owned and operated, owner Basil Daud studied the art of making sausages from his father, and after learning more about the business from his uncle, set out to make lean and healthy products with no unwanted fillers that he could feel good about serving his own children.

Daud’s goal is to help make a difference in the health of British Columbians by offering high quality locally-grown and delicious meats.

Established in 2010, Dawood’s Meat is committed to a higher standard in meat and is located in Surrey, British Columbia.
