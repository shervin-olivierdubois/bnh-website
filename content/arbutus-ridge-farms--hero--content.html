Bring Nutrition Home with Arbutus Ridge Farms Vegetarian Lasagna

Made in the Cowichan Valley, the Provence of Canada, on Vancouver Island, this delicious vegetarian lasagna is prepared with our own tomato sauce of diced tomatoes and braised fresh onions, carrots, celery, garlic and parsley. Add in our béchamel made from scratch, plenty of ricotta, parmesan mozzarella cheeses and eggplant and you’ve got a slice of heaven.

Best of all, this creamy, rich flavoured lasagna also meets BC’s Healthy Eating Guidelines!

We make our lasagna with all fresh ingredients with No Added Preservatives.
