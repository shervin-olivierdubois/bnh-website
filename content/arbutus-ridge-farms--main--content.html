About Arbutus Ridge Farms

Arbutus Ridge Farms Ltd. produces more than 60 food products for retailers across British Columbia and Alberta.

Their products are made using fresh, local and healthy ingredients wherever possible, many are sourced from Vancouver Island and the Fraser Valley. Buying local plays a main role in their products being as fresh as possible; as well as supporting B.C. food producers and reducing their carbon footprint.
