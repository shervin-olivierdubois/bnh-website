About Arbutus Ridge Farms

Arbutus Ridge Farms uses fresh, local and healthy ingredients wherever possible and source many of their ingredients from Vancouver Island and the Fraser Valley. Buying local plays a main role in their products being as fresh as possible; as well as supporting B.C. food producers and reducing their carbon footprint.

Arbutus Ridge Farms sets high standards with their expanding product line. They have salads for every taste and diet, including bean salads, dessert salads, grain and rice salads, pasta salads, potato salads and vegetable salads. Their dips and spreads give you fresh, wholesome appetizers to choose from and their sauces will finish your meals with extra kick. Their hot dish items add fresh-out-of-the-oven goodness to any meal.

Arbutus Ridge Farms "Fresh Deli Foods" was founded in the Cowichan Valley on Vancouver Island in 1997.
